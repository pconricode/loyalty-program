What is the C-A-L Ranch Loyalty Program?
THE C-A-L RANCH LOYALTY PROGRAM IS A WAY OF REWARDING OUR AWESOME, LOYAL CUSTOMERS! FOR EVERY DOLLAR YOU SPEND AT C-A-L RANCH STORES YOU EARN 1 LOYALTY POINT. ONCE YOU EARN A TOTAL OF 500 LOYALTY POINTS, YOU EARNED $5 LOYALTY DOLLARS THAT CAN BE USED IN ANY OF OUR STORES. LOYALTY POINTS AND LOYALTY DOLLARS CAN BE BANKED THROUGH OUT THE YEAR AND USED ON ANY TRANSACTION IN $5 INCREMENTS. LEARN MORE.

Generally, the next day after you earned your Loyalty Points they will appear in your account. Each night our system will take your transaction(s) from the day and upload them into our system.

Any cashier can let you know the balance of your Loyalty Points at any time. Once you have accumulated enough Loyalty Points for an in-store credit you can elect to accumulate Loyalty Points or use them on your purchase.

Loyalty Points and Loyalty Dollars expire on December 31 at 11:59pm MST each year so be sure to use your Loyalty Points and dollars prior to this time.
